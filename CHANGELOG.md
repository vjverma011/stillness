# Changelog

All notable changes to **Stillness** (Meditation Timer) will be documented in this file.

## [0.1] - 2026-02-28

### Timer
- Set meditation durations from 1 second up to 12 hours.
- Custom duration picker with hours, minutes, and seconds.
- Quick-add buttons (+15s, +30s, +1m, +5m) to fine-tune your session length.
- Preset quick-select buttons for 5m, 15m, 30m, and 1h sessions.

### Background Timer & Notifications
- Timer keeps running when you leave the app or lock your screen.
- Persistent notification shows remaining time with a progress bar.
- Pause/Resume and Stop buttons directly in the notification.
- Tap the notification to return to the app.
- Notification updates to "Session Complete" when your session ends.

### Vibration Patterns
- 7 vibration patterns to choose from, in two categories:
  - **Continuous** (loops until you dismiss): Gentle Pulse, Quick Pulse, Wave, Escalating.
  - **Auto-stop** (plays and then goes silent): Gentle Bells, Triple Chime, Fade Out.
- Tap any pattern in Settings to preview it before selecting.

### Settings
- Choose your preferred vibration pattern with a tap-to-preview selector.
- Switch between Dark and Light themes.
- Your preferences are saved and remembered between sessions.

### Look & Feel
- Full dark and light theme support.
- Responsive layout that adapts to different screen sizes and devices.
- Animated circular timer with a pulsing effect while running.
- Clean, bordered buttons throughout the app for a polished look.
- Smooth animations when starting, pausing, and completing sessions.
